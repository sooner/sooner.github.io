Bio

I am now an Associate Professor at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT-CAS). I got my Ph.D. degree in January 2016 in Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Professor Tao Huang. I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science from University of Sciences and Technology of China in 2011.
Research interests:
My research research interests include operating systems, distributed systems, cloud computing and now with a focus on software defined cloud computing.
